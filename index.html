WHO ARE WE..?

Name of Organization

The name of the Organization is NATIONAL NETWORK FOR ELITE WOMEN. in short form called NEWN founded by eight (8) persons.

The term "Elite Women"

“Elite women” Women who possessed Bachelor degree, Masters degree, Doctor of philosophy (PhD) and Professors

Members of NEWN

Membership is Tanzanian elite woman who has attained the age of 18 years and above of sound mind and who is ready to join hands.

Purpose of NEWN

To address the concerns and issues of elite women within Tanzania, who succumb to several social, cultural and economic challenges.

MISSION AND VISION

VISION

"Recognition of contribution(s) of elite women in building the national health and economy."

MISSION

"To have a voice of elite women that realize their potentials and utilize them fully in building the national healthy and economy."

OBJECTIVES

1. To enhance the skills and expertise of elite women.
2. To advocate for challenges facing elite women.
3. To create awareness to the elite women
4. To serve as a service center for justice of young girls.
